Lake Como - Italy

Lake Como is one of Italy's dreamiest lake destinations

It’s hard to think of Lake Como without thinking of its famous residents:
Clooney, Versace, Messi, Madonna. At one point or another, they’ve all
staked their claim to a piece of lakeside real estate here. And one can hardly
blame them for coveting a sliver of Como’s fabulous shoreline. No place in
Italy has quite the cache of Lake Como, and its reputation as the epitome of the
good life is well-deserved.

Santorini, Greece

One of the most beautiful places on Earth is frequently considered to be the lovely
Greek island of Santorini. A volcanic explosion produced a spectacular environment,
which is encircled by recognizable white-washed structures with blue-domed roofs.
Legendary Santorini sunsets cover the cliffs and the Aegean Sea in a warm, golden tint.
You'll come across quaint villages like Oia and Fira as you meander through its winding cobblestone alleyways,
where you can savour delectable Greek cuisine while taking in breathtaking views.
